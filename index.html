Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting

This platform empowers citizens to actively participate in improving their city by reporting civic issues directly to government authorities.

By using technology and community participation, the system ensures transparency, accountability, and faster resolution of problems.

Authorities can analyze issue trends, prioritize resources, and enhance public service delivery using centralized data.

Common Civic Issues

  • Potholes
  • Garbage Overflow
  • Broken Street Lights
  • Water Leakage
  • Open Drains
  • Unsafe Public Places
