Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting

This platform empowers citizens to actively participate in improving their city by reporting real-time civic issues directly to authorities.

By using technology and community participation, the system ensures transparency, accountability, and faster issue resolution, helping build safer and smarter urban environments.

Government departments can efficiently manage complaints, track performance, and analyze problem-prone areas using centralized data.

Common Civic Issues

  • Potholes
  • Garbage Overflow
  • Broken Street Lights
  • Water Leakage
  • Open Drains
  • Unsafe Public Places

About Us

The Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting & Resolution System is a digital governance initiative aimed at strengthening communication between citizens and government bodies. Our goal is to ensure transparent, efficient, and timely resolution of civic problems using technology.
